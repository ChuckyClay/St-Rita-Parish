About the Parish

Parish History

St. Rita Parish has been serving the community for over 50 years. Founded in 1975, our parish has grown from a small congregation to a vibrant community dedicated to faith, service, and fellowship. We are committed to upholding the teachings of the Catholic Church and providing spiritual guidance to all who seek it.

Patron Saint

Our patron saint is St. Rita of Cascia, known as the "Saint of the Impossible" for her unwavering faith and perseverance. She is the patron saint of difficult marriages, abused women, and impossible causes. Her life inspires us to trust in God's mercy and to seek peace in all circumstances.

Mission and Vision

Mission: To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, to celebrate the sacraments, and to serve our community with love and compassion.

Vision: To be a welcoming parish where all are invited to encounter Christ, grow in faith, and participate in the life of the Church.
