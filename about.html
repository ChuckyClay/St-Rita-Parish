Parish History

We are a community of believers with Christ as our head. As a parish, we are comprised of seven prayer houses. St. Rita Parish serves Nkomo Ward, bordering Kagaene, Kianjai and Ruiri parishes.

Read Parish History

Patron Saint

Saint Rita of Cascia is one of the most loved saints in the Catholic Church. She is known as the Saint of the Impossible, the Patroness of hopeless cases, and a powerful intercessor in family and marital struggles.

Mission and Vision

Mission: To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, celebrate the sacraments, and serve our community with love and compassion.

Vision: To be a welcoming parish where all are invited to encounter Christ, grow in faith, and participate in the life of the Church.
