About the Parish

Parish History

We are a community of believers with Christ as our head. As a parish, we are comprised of seven prayer houses.
St Rita Parish serves Nkomo Ward, bordering Kagaene, Kianjai and Ruiri parishes. Read more.


Patron Saint

Our patron saint is St. Rita of Cascia, known as the "Saint of the Impossible" for her unwavering faith and perseverance. She is the patron saint of difficult marriages, abused women, and impossible causes. Her life inspires us to trust in God's mercy and to seek peace in all circumstances. Read more.


Mission and Vision

Mission: To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, to celebrate the sacraments, and to serve our community with love and compassion.

Vision: To be a welcoming parish where all are invited to encounter Christ, grow in faith, and participate in the life of the Church.
