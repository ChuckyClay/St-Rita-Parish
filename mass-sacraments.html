Sacraments Overview

  • Baptism: The sacrament that initiates us into the Christian faith.
  • Eucharist: The source and summit of Christian life.
  • Confirmation: Strengthens our faith and commitment to Christ.
  • Penance: Reconciliation with God through confession.
  • Matrimony: The sacrament of marriage.
  • Anointing of the Sick: Brings spiritual and physical healing.

For more information or to arrange a sacrament, please contact the parish office.
