Mass & Sacraments

Join us in worship and learn more about the sacraments that sustain Christian life.

Mass Timetable

  • Sunday Masses: 9:00 AM, 11:00 AM
  • Weekday Masses: Monday - Saturday, 7:00 AM
  • Holy Days: As announced

Sacraments Overview

  • Baptism: Initiation into the Christian faith.
  • Eucharist: The source and summit of Christian life.
  • Confirmation: Strengthens our faith and commitment to Christ.
  • Penance: Reconciliation with God through confession.
  • Matrimony: The sacrament of Christian marriage.
  • Anointing of the Sick: Prayer, healing, and spiritual strength.
